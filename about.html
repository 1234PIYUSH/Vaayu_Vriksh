About Us

Vision

To revolutionize urban sustainability by merging biotechnology and smart infrastructure, creating cleaner, greener, and healthier cities for future generations.

Our Mission

Improving air quality while promoting sustainability, combining innovation with economic growth for healthier, greener cities.

Our Product & Services

Our Idea

Our company is an innovative urban sustainability startup dedicated to improving air quality and urban infrastructure. Recognizing the limitations of traditional green infrastructure in high-density urban areas, we have developed a cutting-edge solution: Liquid Trees designed to act as both a pollution-filtering system and a functional street bench.
